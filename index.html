About

I’m JC — a creative technologist who builds the whole thing, not just the part you see. Hand me a rough idea and I’ll take it from sketch to something running on a screen, a phone, or a circuit board.

7 years in, I’m drawn to the problems that refuse to stay in one lane: an interface that needs a real backend, an app that has to talk to hardware, a prototype that has to grow up into a product.
